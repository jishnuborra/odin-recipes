FIRE Chocolate Chip Cookies

Very good very big chocolate chip cookies 10/10 would make several times

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks salted butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2.5 cups all purpose flour
  • 12 oz chocolate chips (or more)

How to Make

  1. Melt the butter completely before mixing in the sugar, molasses, eggs and vanilla until smooth
  2. Mix in the baking soda and two cups of the flour.
  3. Add more flour until dough appears wet but is not terribly sticky.
  4. Refrigerate dough for at least 30 min
  5. Preheat oven to 375 Fahrenheit
  6. Roll dough into size of golf balls and bake
